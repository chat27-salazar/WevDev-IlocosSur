About Ilocos Sur
Ilocos Sur is a province in the Ilocos Region of the Philippines known for its rich cultural heritage, well-preserved colonial-era architecture, and vibrant traditions. Home to Vigan, the only UNESCO World Heritage City in Asia outside of Japan and China.
